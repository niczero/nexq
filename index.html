Summary

An asynchronous job execution engine, inspired by Resque and SideKiq.

Trying it out

$ eval `perl -Mlocal::lib=/tmp/rexq`
$ cpanm -l /tmp/rexq https://github.com/niczero/rexq.git

Authors and Contributors

Diego Kuperman wrote http://diegok.github.com/resque-perl, a perl port of http://defunkt.github.com/resque. Diego appears on irc://irc.perl.org/mojo as diegok and has taken time to explain some of his design decisions. His code is on CPAN as Resque (http://metacpan.org/module/Resque) and he has been using it in production since late 2011.
